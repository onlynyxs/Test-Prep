💡 Strategic Insights

Based on our analysis of NEB Grade 12 Mathematics past year questions (PYQs), the updated syllabus emphasizes a balanced approach between conceptual understanding and computational techniques.

  • Calculus: The highest weightage domain. Expect definite integration properties, area under curves, and exact/linear differential equations in Group C.
  • Algebra: Matrix inversion and Cramer's rule are frequent. De Moivre's theorem and Binomial expansions alternate in long answer sections.
  • Comp. Math: The Simplex method (LPP maximization) is heavily favored for Group C (8 marks). Numerical methods (Newton-Raphson, Simpson's 1/3) dominate Group B.
